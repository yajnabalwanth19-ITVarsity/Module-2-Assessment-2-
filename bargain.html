Established in 1998, Bargain Books is South Africa’s leading discount book retailer with 82 stores in all 9 provinces. Our mission is to provide our customers with quality and innovative products, value for money and excellent service. We have an extensive product offering, many exclusive to Bargain Books and also publish Afrikaans Children’s Books.Bargain Books has long been devoted to delivering on four unique and particular strengths: range, convenience, service and value.
